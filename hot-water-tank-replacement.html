The benefits of a tankless water heater

  • Much more energy efficient as they heat up water on-demand
  • You can save around 25% on your annual operation costs
  • It lasts twice as long (likely twice as long than a tank system).
  • Never run out hot water
  • Uses less space

Cons Of Tankless Water Heater Systems

  • Way more expensive to install
  • Buys cost more
  • Hot water may take longer to get

Direct venting water heaters use an electric blower with separate chambers to funnel outside air to the heater and to vent the exhaust back outside. They are used in areas without sufficient ventilation -- for example, in a bathroom.

Direct Venting with Power

(starting from $38.68/month)

Direct vent power water heaters work in the same way as direct vent units but use a 2-pipe system that draws outside air in to heat it and then exhausts out. They are typically used in areas where there isn't enough airflow to support combustion.
